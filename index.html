About this Application

Selecting the "Customer" menu option will return a list of customers including the customer ID, company name, and city.

Selecting the "Order History" menu option allows you to access a list of products ordered by a particular customer by entering
the customer ID.

You can access a customer's order history by clicking the link for each company name in the customer list.

You can also access a a customer's current orders by clicking "Current Orders" button for a particular customer in the customer list.

You can update the information for a particular order by clicking the "Update Order" button in the current orders list for a particular customer.
