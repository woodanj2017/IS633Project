About this Application

1.) Selecting the "Customer" menu option will return a list of customers
including the customer ID, company name, and city.

2.) Selecting the "Order History" menu option allows you to access a list
of products ordered by a particular customer by entering
the customer ID.

3.) You can also access a customer's order history by clicking the link
for each company name in the customer list.
